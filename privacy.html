Privacy Policy

Developer [Meitel] ("the Developer") respects the privacy of users of the smartphone application "Resonance AI" ("the App") and is committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information Collection and Purpose

  • Input Text Data: Used to generate appropriate AI responses (advice) and provide in-app features like "World Resonance."
  • User Profile: Information such as nickname, age group, gender, and location (city/country level) is used for personalizing AI advice and community features.
  • Usage Data: Statistical data, such as the number of happiness shares, is aggregated to improve the App's community features.

2. Children's Privacy (Under 13)

This App is designed to be safe for children under 13. When a child uses the App, the following applies:

  • The Developer does not collect more personal information than necessary.
  • We recommend that children use the App under the supervision of a parent or guardian.

3. Third-Party Services

This App uses the following third-party services, and data is processed according to their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Gemini API: Text data is sent to Google's servers for AI response generation.
  • Firebase (Google Cloud): Used for data synchronization and storage.
  • Google Speech Recognition: Used to convert voice input into text.

4. Data Management and Deletion

Users can manage or delete their data through the App's settings or by contacting the Developer.
